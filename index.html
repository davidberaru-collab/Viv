About the School

Apostolic Alignment School of Ministry helps emerging and established leaders grow in theology, character, and ministry practice. Our faculty and mentors are committed to raising up servant-leaders who carry biblical conviction and spiritual clarity.

"Study to show yourself approved" is more than a verse to us—it is a discipleship model for lifelong growth.
